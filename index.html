Mathematics and Computer Science

Inventing tomorrow - through computational sciences and robotics The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science (MCS) invites students to prepare for some of the most coveted jobs and most important advances in technology today, by pursuing the degrees we offer in computer science, applied mathematics, and robotics:

  • BS in computer science

  • BS in applied and computational mathematics

  • MS in computational sciences and robotics

Our BS computer science degree is accredited by the Computing Accreditation Commission of ABET, http://www.abet.org

Our department takes great pride in our degree and active research programs that span a variety of disciplines. The department values high-quality teaching and we currently have five faculty members that have earned awards for excellence in teaching. We also provide many unique experiences where students can readily participate in a research projects, compete in academic competitions, or join one of our professional society student chapters.

Our department is expanding and seeks new faculty members in Computer Science with an expected start date of fall semester, 2015. Find out more.
